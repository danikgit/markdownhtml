How To Make A Sandwich
  1. Get a plate
  2. Pick two delicious slices of bread to your liking
  3. Lay your desired slice of cheese on one bread slice
  4. Add your deli meat and toppings such as lettuce or tomatoes on top of the cheese
  5. Add your condiments, Mayonnaise, mustard, (I prefer spicy mustard)
  6. Take a bite and enjoy!
